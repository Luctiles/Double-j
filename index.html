Welcome to School Care & Learn

At School Care & Learn Junior School Ngogwe, we provide a nurturing, child-centred environment where curiosity is celebrated and every learner is supported to reach their full potential. Our dedicated team, led by Headmaster Nakafero Sarah, blends academic excellence with character building and joyful discovery.

We invite you to explore our campus, meet our teachers, and become part of the Ngogwe family. For admissions, visits, or any inquiries, reach out to us — we're here to help.
